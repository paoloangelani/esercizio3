Focaccia with caramelized onion, cranberries, and brie

wow your taste buds with focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions,
tart cranberries, and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the Topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tables olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not
    necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough
